The Mathematics Statistics Competition is held every year and it brings together a large number of teams from Secondary Schools from the Wider Whanganui, Manawatu district. Students are selected and placed into teams based on their Mathematical ability and enthusiasm for challenges. The format of the competition changes every year, but a broad range of Mathematical skills and quick thinking is a huge advantage. 

**Who can join:** Year 12 students.

**Event:** M3S: Massey Manawatu Maths and Stats Competition (Massey University) at the end of Term 2.
