On Monday 18 May, a group of our international students, exchange students and Head Prefects had the privilege of attending the Mayor’s Welcome to Whanganui, hosted by Andrew Tripe at the Cooks Gardens Function Centre.

The event brought together student leaders and international students from across Whanganui to connect, share experiences and engage in meaningful discussions about life in our city. Students were also given the opportunity to provide feedback and ideas on what could make their experience in Whanganui even better.

A big thank you to Whanganui District Council and Whanganui & Partners  for organising such a welcoming and valuable event for our young people.
