Big day at Dannevirke Equestrian Interschools for our riders Stella, Olivia, and Lily, yesterday coming away as team champions for show jumping with over 100 competitors. 

Olivia smashed it out of the park placing 1st in both the 90cm AM5 and 90cm Table C. 

Lily had 2 beautiful rounds placing 4th in her 1.05m AM5 and 7th in the 1.05m Table C. 

Well done team, a top effort.
