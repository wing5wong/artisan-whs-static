<h4><span>Snap! Meghan and Kerry with the Karen Walker designed coat.</span></h4>
<h4 class="element element-paragraph">It's a coat that has been turning heads as the royal tour gathers pace ... and one of the designers behind it is none other than Whanganui's Kerry Ranginui (former WHS student).</h4>
<p class="element element-paragraph">The striking trench coat worn by Meghan, Duchess of Sussex, as she accompanies Prince Harry on their visit to New Zealand, has caught the eye of fashion gurus around the world.</p>

<p class="element element-paragraph">It is the work of iconic Kiwi designer label Karen Walker, and its updated look is down to pattern-maker Ranginui who works as part of the Walker team in Auckland.</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">Ranginui, making his mark as a contestant on the TV fashion competition Project Runway NZ, took to social media to celebrate the royal connection.</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">"Great team at Karen Walker working on the coat for the Duchess of Sussex arrival to nz," he posted.</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">Ranginui also posted a photo of himself dressed in the coat saying: "The Duchess of Sussex trench coat!!! Pre-order now sold out in one day!"</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">He revealed to the Chronicle that Meghan had a long connection with Karen Walker clothes going back to her days as an actress.</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">"The coat's a Karen Walker design that has been part of the collection for a very long time ... since around 2003," he said, explaining that he and others in the team had updated the brown tweed trench coat.</p>

<p class="element element-paragraph">"Meghan Markle was wearing Karen Walker designs before she became the Duchess of Sussex.</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">"Now that she is so much more high profile it means that people want to buy what she's wearing so, of course, there have been a lot of orders for the coat."</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">He said the Duchess was wearing Karen Walker again on Tuesday morning.</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">"The blazer was patterned by me and made by the team."</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">Ranginui again passed muster with the Project Runway NZ judges in Monday night's episode.</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">He was delighted for his fellow contestant Judy Gao who was the wowed the judges with her Holden Astra inspired black dress and was named the winner.</p>
