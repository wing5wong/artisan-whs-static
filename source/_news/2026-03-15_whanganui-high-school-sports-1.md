Action from the field 📸🔥 We were thrilled to host the Whanganui Secondary Schools Athletics Championships at Cooks Gardens last week, with 315 rangatahi registered from 8 schools across the region competing in 50 track and 35 field events throughout the day.

Huge thanks to our volunteer helpers from Athletics Wanganui Inc and all contributing schools.
