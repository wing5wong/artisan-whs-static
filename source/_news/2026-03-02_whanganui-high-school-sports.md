Congratulations to Noah Frederick on his debut Mixed Martial Arts fight in Hastings on Saturday. Noah had a tough fight but mixed the striking with several takedowns and submission attempts which made for one of the most exciting fights of the day. Noah came away with a decision win and he is now looking forward to his next fight.
