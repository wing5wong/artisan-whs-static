Awesome morning on the Awa for our rowing squad racing in the annual Dragon Boat Race and AWRC opening day Mitchell Shield.

10 crews given 1 hour to decorate their boats, then on the water to race for a double title - Dragon Boat winner and AWRC Mitchell shield winner 2025. 

Congrats to the winning team 

Lily & Jake Newton, Quin & Milly Vivian with

Coxswains Samantha Kenny. 

Best Dressed Boat/Team was Nicky Maxim, Callum Morgan, Ava Overton & Myiah Dudson 

Thanks to Mr Ding on behalf of the WHS confucius classroom for sponsoring the prizes
