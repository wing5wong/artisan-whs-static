On Tuesday 23 June we hosted our annual sports exchange with New Plymouth Girls’ High School, and it was a fantastic day of competition and sportsmanship. Our students represented Whanganui High School with determination and enthusiasm across all codes.

Results• Hockey – WHS won 3–0Player of the Day: Amy Davidson 

• A1 Netball – WHS won 37–20Player of the Day: Elicia Thompson 

• JA1 Netball – WHS won 27–23Player of the Day: Olive Coombs 

• Basketball – WHS lost 68–21Player of the Day: Elicia Thompson 

• Football 1st XI – WHS lost 3–2Player of the Day: Shino Takano 

• Football 2nd XI – Draw 1–1Player of the Day: Chelsie Howe 

• Rugby – WHS lost 58–17Player of the Day: Moana Newland 

A huge thank you to all of our coaches, managers, officials, scorers, and supporters whose hard work helped make the day such a success. We would also like to extend a special thank you to Larsen Whittaker and Caleb Christison for capturing the action and memories of the day through their photography.Up the Swamp!
