Whanganui High School MTB students went away in April to the 

Schools Mountain Bike Festival in Manawatu. 

Our boys did exceptionally well over the weekend. 

Denby Whittington raced the Downhill on Sunday winning the u16 and also won king of the hill with the fastest time of the day!

Julian Dunn won u15

Harvey Harris won u20

Lucas Spink won u17

Reon Humphries 7th in u16

Well done to you all.
