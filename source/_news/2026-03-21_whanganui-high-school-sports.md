Huge congratulations to our WHS Green team for taking out the Year 9/10 Rippa Rugby competition on Friday! After six weeks of fantastic rippa, it was an all-WHS grand final, with WHS Green edging out WHS Gold by just one try in a thrilling finish.

A big thank you to Whanganui Rugby for running such an awesome competition. The players are already looking forward to the next one. And judging by the excitement, everyone was keen to get a photo with the trophy 🤣

Group photo is of both WHS teams 💚💛

Many thanks to Michelle Tamehana and Regan Bourke for helping with the teams also 🙏🙏
