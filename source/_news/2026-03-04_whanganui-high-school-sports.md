Amazing start for our WHS Athletics squad who are at the NZ Track & Field Nationals in Auckland this weekend. 

 Auguz Thongskul silver medal U18 long jump, and through to the final of 100mtrs. 

Bruce McGregor silver in 300mtr hurdles and James McGregor a bronze, plus ex WHS student Damian Hodgson won the senior men's 400mtr hurdles.

Good luck for day 2.
