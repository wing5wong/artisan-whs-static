A big ‘shout out’ for two outstanding performances from Ethan Toy and Noah Toy for the 1st XI cricket team this season 🏏

A special mention to Ethan Toy, who produced two centuries this season against Stratford and Palmerston North Boys’ High School! An awesome achievement.

Equally impressive was Noah Toy, who delivered a phenomenal innings on Wednesday against Palmerston North Boys’, scoring 128 runs off just 40 balls! It was an outstanding display of power hitting and composure.

As a team, the boys continue to perform strongly in the Manawatu McVicar Cup, currently sitting at 6 wins from 6 games, with final matches to be played in Term 4. This follows on from a 3rd place finish at the Ross Taylor Bat Tournament.

Well done, Ethan & Noah! 💚💛💚
