Welcome to Whanganui High School! 🌏🎉

Today we proudly welcomed 24 new international and exchange students to our school. These amazing students have joined us from Cambodia, Czech Republic, Germany, Japan, the United States of America, and Viet Nam.

We’re so excited to have you as part of our school whānau. Your journey here will be filled with new friendships, learning opportunities, and unforgettable experiences — and we’re here to support you every step of the way. 🌟

To all our new students: welcome to Whanganui High School — your new home away from home. We wish you all the very best for your time here!
