And they are off to the UK…. Wishing Y13 student Nicky Maxim, Ex WHS students Bryn Morgan and Jake Newton and coach Axel Dickinson all the very best for their Henley Rowing Regatta in England. 

The Henley Royal Regatta is the world's most famous rowing regatta, held annually on the River Thames in England. Founded in 1839, it is a highlight of the British summer social season, combining elite international sport with strict traditional dress codes.

Racing starts June 30th through to July 5th.
