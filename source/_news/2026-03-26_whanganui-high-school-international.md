Our International Students have enjoyed their first day on the trip to Wellington.

A few fun stops along the way,  including the Foxton Windmill and a play at Levin Adventure Park (turns out our staff were just as keen on the playground as the students!).

In Wellington, they visited Te Papa Tongarewa and took in the view from Mount Victoria Lookout.

Looking forward to more adventures tomorrow.
