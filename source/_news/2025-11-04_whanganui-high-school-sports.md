The Whanganui Secondary School Sports Awards 2025 were held at Whanganui Girls College last Wednesday 29th October.  A huge congratulations to the winners from WHS:

Ben Ross - Official of the Year

Lucas Munn - Senior International Sportsman of the Year

Matt Smith - Senior National Sportsman of the Year

Micky Zhang - Junior International Sportsman of the Year

Thanks to Sport Whanganui and Mitre 10 Mega Whanganui for a lovely awards evening.
