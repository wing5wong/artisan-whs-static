New Plymouth Girls’ High School sports exchange is on Tuesday 29 June (away).

Teams involved are:

Junior A1 & A2 Netball

Girls Rugby  
1st XI Girls Hockey  
1st XI Girls Football


Please make sure you are there as information about the day will be given out.
