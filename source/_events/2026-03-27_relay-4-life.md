Take part in Relay For Life!  
Saturday 28 March, 2pm to 10pm

Come along for an hour, 10 mins, or the whole time.  
Bring your friends and family. Dress up in bright colours. We are walking a relay so have to keep the baton moving all the time. 

Come along and support the school and more importantly, support Cancer research.  
It is a gold coin entry at the gate.  

Collect a sponsor form from Mrs de Smidt and get friends and whanau to donate if you do the walk! Hand your sponsor forms and money collected to Mrs de Smidt on the day. 

Please register for on the bitly even if you have already collected a sponsor form. [bit.ly/Relayforlife2026](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSf4bXGhGBLk1UQChBs116R_KQqdlCh0mDpCivEnzZOJTjZY4Q/viewform)
