Market Day is on Friday 19 June at first break, in centre court.

Please remember to bring some cash to school, so you can purchase some delicious kai for lunch from the Y11 Business Studies students. 

There is a wide range on offer including home baking, sausage sizzle, steak sandwiches, hot chips, hot dogs, slushies, hot chocolates, ice cream sundaes and candyfloss.
