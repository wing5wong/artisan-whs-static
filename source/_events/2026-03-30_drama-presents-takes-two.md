Come and support our talented Year 12 Drama students as they take to the stage for their upcoming performance of Takes Two by kiwi author David Hill. The class has been working incredibly hard to bring this script to life, and they would love to see a full house of peers, friends, and family cheering them on.

The Story: A sharp and relatable look at the complexities of teenage life, Takes Two explores the high-stakes drama of relationships and the courage it takes to speak your truth.

Date: Tuesday, 31 March  
Time: 7:00pm (Please arrive early to find a seat!)  
Location: The School Hall  
Entry: Koha (Gold coin donation appreciated at the door)

Whether you’re a drama enthusiast or just looking for a great night of entertainment, come along and celebrate the creativity of our Year 12 cohort.  

See you there!
