All Year 12 students are to report to the Racecourse (just down the road) at 9.15am sharp TODAY for our RYDA Driver Safety Course.  

RYDA is an engaging, interactive programme designed to help you become safer, smarter drivers and passengers. It’s practical, eye-opening and incredibly relevant as many of you begin driving or are close to it.

You will need to bring a pen, Lunch/Snacks and water and be in school uniform please.

The course will finish at 2.00pm.

This is a compulsory Year 12 event and an important part of keeping you, and others - safe on the road. It’s a great day out of the classroom and a valuable life skill experience.
(Students who would normally be on a UCOL course or House Build tomorrow - Mr Marr has let them know that you won't be attending and you are expected to be at RYDA)

See you there!
