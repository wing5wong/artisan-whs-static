##### Start of Term 1, Monday 26 January  
**Year 9 & Year 13, & new students from all other year levels**.

-----

#### **For Year 9 and pupils new to WHS**

**Pre 9.00am**  
Students will assemble on the Purnell St footpath.  
Prefects assisting.

**9.00am**  
**Powhiri** - Staff and Year 13 student leaders welcoming new staff, Year 9 and other students new to the school.  
Morning tea at the conclusion of powhiri. 

**10.15am (approx)**  
**Y9 and other new pupils back to Hall** for organisation into form classes. Taken to form rooms.  
Deans of year groups other than Y9 need to collect the new pupils and provide some orientation.

**11.30am – 12.00pm**  
Lunch  

**12.00pm – 12.10pm**  
In form rooms – leave bags.  

**12.10pm – 2.50pm**  
Activities in House groups.  

**2.50pm** –  
Student, staff and Whanau sausage sizzle.  
End of school day.

---------

**12.00pm - 12.30pm**  
Lunch break.  

**12.30pm - 1.30pm**  
Period 4.  

**1.30pm - 2.30pm**  
Period 5.

Year 13 students can be released at 2.30pm.
