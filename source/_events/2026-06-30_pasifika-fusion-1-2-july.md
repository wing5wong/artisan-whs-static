[Pasifika Fusion 2026](https://www.pasifikafusion.co.nz/) will be held at Regent on Broadway, Palmerston North on Wednesday 1 July and Thursday 2 July.

Students compete in a range of areas such as; wearable art, talent, quizzes, essay writing, speeches and cultural performances. A lot of time and hard work goes into preparing for this two day event, and is a highlight of the year for our Pasifika students. 

-------------------- 

Pasifika Fusion Submissions

Submissions for Pasifika Fusion - Speech, Talent, Wearable Art (blurb), Poetry - are due by 5 June.  Please use the Google Doc in the Pasifika Fusion Google Classroom to make your submissions. I would be confirming the quiz team by next week. Those students who had submitted their names for "talent", check the marking sheets for the requirements and confirm your entries. 

If you have any questions, come and see Ms. Devi in E5 before the deadline.
