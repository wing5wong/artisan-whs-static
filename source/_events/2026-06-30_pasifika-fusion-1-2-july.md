[Pasifika Fusion 2026](https://www.pasifikafusion.co.nz/) will be held at Regent on Broadway, Palmerston North on Wednesday 1 July and Thursday 2 July.












**Program for the 2 days**  
The students will be travelling for the event on both days. The program timings [approximately] are as follows:  

**Wednesday 1 July:** 8.30 am to 6.00pm  
Cultural Performances, Speeches (Eng/PI), Quiz.(in Auditorium & Regency floors), Wearable Art, Visual Art, Poetry, Essay.  

**Thursday 2 July:** 8.30am to 9.30pm  
8.30am to 3pm: Talent category, Speeches (Eng/PI) in Auditorium & Regency floors  
6pm: Night Concert & Prize-Giving.  

**Event/Travel Details**  
The students are travelling in two vans hired from Rite Price Rentals.  
Departure: 7.30 am on both days from WHS bus bay.  
Arrival: Wednesday 7.30pm, Thursday 10.30pm

**Wednesday 1 July**  
Students arrive at WHS bus bay by 7.20 am and depart in the van by 7.30am  
8.30am All students arrive at The Regent on Broadway for the start of the day.  
8.45am-6.00pm Official Opening. Culture Performances, Drama, Talent, Wearable Art
(on stage), display of Science, Essay and Poetry, Quiz, Wearable and Visual Arts.  

Departure from Palmerston North latest by 6pm. The students will contact their
caregivers by phone before we leave Palmerston North with time of arrival.  

**Thursday 2 July**  
Students arrive at WHS bus bay by 7.20am and depart in the van by 7.30am  
8.30am All students arrive at The Regent on Broadway for the start of the day.  
Day program: 8.45am to 3pm [English and PI Speeches]  
Students get a break from 3pm to 5.30pm for tea  

6pm - 9pm Evening Concert followed by prizegiving  

**Departure from Palmerston North**  
At the latest 9.30pm: We will leave for Whanganui High School. We will contact all
families by phone before we leave Palmerston North with time of arrival.  

**Arrival at WHS**  
We will ALL return at approximately **11:00pm on Thursday 2 July**, 2026. The students
can be collected from the WHS Bus Bay.  

**Food:** Students will be provided lunch on Wednesday and Thursday and dinner on Thursday.
We request if students can bring some snacks and water on both days.

If you have any questions, come and see Ms Devi in E5.
