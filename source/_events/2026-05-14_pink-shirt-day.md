PINK SHIRT WEEK IS HERE!!!  
Come and bring your anti-bullying spirit to the Centre Court at First Break today, Monday 11 May and Tuesday 12 May and join in some amazing activities like Mural Chalk and Pink Message Writing.  
Slogan for the week: THINK PINK!

Pink Shirt Day NZ 2026 is on Friday, 15 May 2026, with the campaign focusing on speaking up, standing together, and stopping bullying. Schools, workplaces, and communities wear pink to support mental health, celebrate diversity, and fundraise to prevent bullying.
