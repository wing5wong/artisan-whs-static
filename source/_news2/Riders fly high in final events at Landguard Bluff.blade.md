<p><span>Brothers Ben and Scott Emerson (both WHS students), who both competed in the 13-16 Year 85cc class, were getting air on Sunday.<br />Photos / Supplied</span></p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">After five months of competition, the Wanganui Motorcycle Club's winter motocross series wrapped up at the Landguard Bluff track on Sunday.</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">Competing in mini, junior and senior divisions, all told around 150 riders took part in the series at some stage, facing all the possible weather and track conditions over that time.</p>
<p class="element element-paragraph">The WMCC wished to thank all the family members, supporters and volunteers for their hard work to run the series and maintain the track.</p>
