**Purpose:**

To increase students' understanding of Mathematics and build their confidence in using Mathematics to solve practical problems.

**Content:**

The course will continue to develop the students' knowledge of Number, Measurement, Algebra, Probability, Statistics and Geometry. Trigonometry may be introduced. NCEA Level 1 Numeracy Standard may be offered to students who are ready for assessment.


**Assessment:**

Common topic tests on content and process. End of year examination.
